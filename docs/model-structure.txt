Courses
Our e-learning platform will offer courses on various subjects. Each course will be divided into a configurable number of modules, and each module will contain a configurable number of contents. There will be contents of various types: text, file, image, or video

The data structure of our course catalog will look like:
    Subject 1
        Course 1
            Module 1
                Content 1 (image)
                Content 2 (text)
            Module 2
                Content 3 (text)
                Content 4 (file)
                Content 5 (video)
    ...
